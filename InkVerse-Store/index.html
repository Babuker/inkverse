Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay with USDT?

Use any TRC20 compatible wallet (Trust Wallet, Binance, etc.) to send USDT to our address. Make sure to select TRC20 network during the transaction.

When will I receive my book?

After sending payment and the transaction hash via WhatsApp, we'll deliver your digital book within 24 hours (usually within 1-2 hours).

What if I send to wrong network?

We only accept TRC20. If you send via another network (ERC20, BEP20), we cannot recover your funds. Please double-check before sending!

What format are the books?

All books are delivered in PDF and EPUB formats, compatible with any e-reader, tablet, or computer. Some titles also include MOBI format for Kindle.

Can I get a refund?

Due to the digital nature of our products, we don't offer refunds. However, if you don't receive your book within 24 hours, contact us via WhatsApp.

How many times can I download?

You get unlimited downloads for all purchased books. The download link will be sent to your email and will remain active indefinitely.
